About Me

Learn more about who I am

I am a student at Brigham Young University with a strong interest in data analytics, business, and leadership. I enjoy working in team environments and solving complex problems through both analytical and creative approaches.

Through my work experience at Great Harvest and The Creamery on Ninth, I have developed strong customer service, communication, and time management skills. I have also held leadership roles in youth organizations, where I planned events, supported others, and helped guide group progress.
